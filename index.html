About us

Welcome! In our notebook you can organize all aspects of your life! You will be able to:
1. create notes;
2. keep a personal diary protected with your password;
3. create a shopping list and share it;
4. fill out the list of affairs for the current day as well as for the future.
A nice bonus is the ability to keep a mood diary with the addition of notes and track the graph of your mood.
